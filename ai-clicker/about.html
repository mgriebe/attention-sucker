About Attention Sucker

Attention Sucker is a satirical incremental clicker game that explores the dark side of AI-generated content, bot automation, and the collapse of the digital attention economy.

What is Attention Sucker?

Attention Sucker is a free browser-based idle game where you start by manually posting AI-generated content for profit. As you progress, you automate the process with bots, battle detection systems, manage mask economics, and watch reality itself deteriorate under the weight of synthetic content.

The game satirizes modern issues including:

  • AI Content Farms - The rise of mass-produced AI slop flooding social media
  • Bot Automation - The arms race between content bots and detection systems
  • Attention Economy - The commodification of human attention and engagement
  • Digital Authenticity - The erosion of trust, quality, and reality coherence
  • Market Saturation - Economic collapse from content oversupply

How to Play

  1. Click - Start by clicking "POST AI SLOP" to earn money
  2. Upgrade - Buy AutoPoster Bots to automate posting
  3. Scale - Purchase upgrades and multipliers to increase production
  4. Defend - Once you hit 30 posts/second, platforms start detecting bots
  5. Mask Up - Use Bot Masks to protect your bots from detection
  6. Expand - Unlock AI Image Posters, Video Posters, and more advanced systems
  7. Survive - Manage the infection system, market value, and reality coherence

Technical Details

Attention Sucker is built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript - no frameworks, no dependencies. It features:

  • Local save system with import/export functionality
  • Dynamic background colors that shift with game progression
  • Material Symbols icons for visual bot representation
  • Mask duration system with immunity periods
  • Spatial infection spreading mechanics
  • Production-based stage progression (not money-based)
  • Mutagenic Mask upgrade system with exponential stacking
